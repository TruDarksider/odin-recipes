Momma's Terrible Lasagna

Description

You can't mess this goodness up. You can travel to Italy, but you'll never find this pasta dish anywhere else but home.

In fact, it's so unique that this is the only place in time you can find this dish you're about to make. Enjoy the journey and remember: this is the only time this dish will ever be made.

Ingredients

  • Premade noodles
  • Prejarred sauce
  • Prepackaged meat
  • Preshredded cheese

Steps

  1. Follow box instructions for making the noodles.
  2. You did buy lasagna noodles, right?
  3. Make meat according to package instructions.
  4. Layer items in an oven-safe-ish dish in order of sauce, noodles, meat, cheese.
  5. Continue layering until you've run out of pasta, sauce, meat, AND cheese.
  6. Bake in a 325-450 degree oven until sauce bubbles on side or top is golden brown.
